## 🛡️ Public Smart Contract Risk Snapshots

**Accessible • Technical • Evidence-Based**

---

## 📌 What This Repository Is

This repository publishes **low-cost, independent technical risk snapshots** for blockchain tokens and smart contracts.

The purpose is to improve **risk awareness before interaction** with unknown contracts — especially for users who cannot justify the cost of full professional audits.

These reports are **not trading signals**, **not investment advice**, and **not recommendations**.
They describe **observable smart contract behavior** at a specific point in time.

---

## 🎯 Intended Audience

This project is designed for:

* 👤 Individual users and small participants
* 🧑‍💻 Independent developers
* 🪙 Early or cautious users
* 🔍 Anyone seeking technical clarity **before interacting** with a smart contract

It is **not intended** for:

* Trading strategies
* Price speculation
* Investment recommendations
* Marketing or promotional claims

---

## 🧭 How These Reports Are Meant to Be Used

These reports are **technical inputs**, not conclusions.

They provide structured findings that help users:

* Detect common scam and honeypot patterns
* Identify transfer restrictions or asymmetric control
* Understand technical risk concentration
* Recognize behaviors inconsistent with safe interaction
* Avoid blind interaction with unknown contracts

**Final interpretation and decisions always remain with the user.**

---

## 🔐 Risk Awareness — Why These Reports Exist

Interacting with a smart contract always carries risk.

A full report does **not** provide guarantees, recommendations, or outcomes.

Instead, it serves as a **low-cost technical risk-awareness tool**, designed to reduce blind interaction by exposing:

* Conditions under which interaction may be unsafe
* Structural patterns commonly used in scams
* Hidden execution controls or restrictions
* Wallet-draining or asymmetric behaviors

The price of an extended report should be understood as an **information cost paid before exposure**, not as payment for advice or protection.
